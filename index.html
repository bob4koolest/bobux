About Bobux Hotel

The roots of Bobux Hotel began in the year 69 AD, when the Romans discovered the location of the almighty Bobux Man. The Bobux Man promised to provide food and shelter to the Romans at an affordable rate as he possessed the world's supply of this valuable resource, and so Bobux Hotel began. The tradition of affordable yet luxurious travel and lodging continues to this day, and new technologies are constantly being developed by the Bobux Man to make your travels a bit safer, cheaper, yet more comfortable.

Why choose us?

Affordable

The Bobux Man is very generous and is dedicated to making travel more affordable. He accepts payment in Bobux only, but offers free exchange services from any currency.

Luxurious

Every room has a ultra-fast BobloxNet™ connection, a 420k TV, and 5 king-sized beds, starting at only 9 bobux per night! The hotel provides all 3 meals, and the Bobux Man himself will serve you breakfast and deliver it to your room. He also provides unlimited foot massages.

Unique and Fun

As the Bobux Man is a very wealthy and cultured individual, he has many bobux generators, enabling you to play your favorite Boblox™ games and make unlimited purchases using the BobuxNet™ currency.
